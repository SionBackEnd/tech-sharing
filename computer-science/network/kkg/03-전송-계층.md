## Multiplexing and Demultiplexing

### Demultiplexing

- Connectionless demux (= UDP)

  - Dest IP, Dest Port로 판단

- Connection-oriented demux (= TCP)

  - Dest IP, Dest Port, Src IP, Src Port로 판단

## UDP: Segment header

`Source Port #`, `Dest Port #`, `Length`, `Checksum`
